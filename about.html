What is Mitheria

Mitheria is the division that is currently in power in our world. Formerly a military focused regime, the last two hundred years have seen incredible technological advances.

Through the hardwork of Mitherian citizens we have created weapons to fight agains the creatures that lurk in the dark, and while not a word most of us enjoied to use for the longest time, the monsters. this is also why we are contacting you, if we are correct in travel through the pockets your world might also be in trouble. And we truly wish to start an alliance based on respect and kin-ship with all of you. So heed our warnings, if you see the monsters that we write about here, please run. Your atmosphere should weaken them to the point where they are barely able to exist, but these creatures they are quite adaptable and we fear that they can overcome this.
